* Publications: [ADS library](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/public-libraries/tY0f2oP7SvO5yRfNlp4aZw)
* Solar flare models: [F-CHROMA database](https://star.pst.qub.ac.uk/wiki/doku.php/public/solarmodels/start)
